Please follow the DRC as outlined below. If these rules are violated, your PCB will not be printed.

    Minimum clearance(track to track) - 0.3 mm
    Minimum track width - 0.3 mm
    Minimum track-pad clearance - 0.3 mm
    Minimum connection width - 0.3 mm
    Minimum copper to hole clearance - 0.4 mm
    Minimum copper to edge clearance - 2 mm
    Minimum pad-pad clearance - 0.4mm
    Minimum through hole - 0.4mm
    Minimum annular width - 0.2mm
    Hole to hole clearance - 1.5 mm
    A sharp bend of 90 degrees or an acute bend in the tracks is NOT allowed.
    The design must be of a single layer. No VIAs are allowed.
